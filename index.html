Yes. TaskTimeline Pro has a free plan that includes 1 project and 10 tasks — no credit card required. Paid plans start at $19/month with a 14-day free trial included.
Is this a good Asana or Monday.com alternative?▼
Yes. TaskTimeline Pro focuses on Gantt-style visual timelines. Asana charges $24.99/user/month for Gantt access — that's $250/month for a 10-person team. TaskTimeline Pro's Team plan is $49/month flat, over 5x cheaper.
Can I share my project timeline with clients?▼
Yes. Every project has a shareable link. Share a read-only view with anyone — no account required to view. Perfect for client reporting.
Do I need to install anything?▼
No. TaskTimeline Pro is 100% web-based. It works in any modern browser on desktop or mobile. Nothing to install, no app to download.
Can I export my data?▼
Yes. Export to CSV, PDF, or JSON at any time on paid plans. Your data is never locked in.
